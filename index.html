About Us

Hello, my name is Paras and I am the leader of the Conservative Party. Our party is dedicated to making Canada a great place for all citizens by having these great platforms: Healthcare: Boosting healthcare funding and mental health services for Canadians Jobs: Creating 1 million jobs if elected as well as providing friendly loans to small to medium businesses Housing: Building 1 million new houses using 15% of government property and starting a housing scheme for indigenous peoples and Tax: removing carbon tax with an alternate “green savings account,” which is money put into an account that can be used to buy “green” items. This is the way we can push Canada forward and be one of the fastest growing economies in the world. Regards, Paras Sharma, leader of the Conservative Party.
